fiabotzolemoana

fiabotz: slang for 'know it all' from the word fiapoto (Sāmoa). Fia as in to desire or 'wannabe' and poto as in intelligence / smarts. Generally used by Sāmoans living in Aotearoa.

o-le-moana: of the sea or ocean.

bot (short for "robot"): an automated program that runs over the Internet. Some bots run automatically, while others respond to specific input. There are many different types of bots such as web crawlers, chat room bots, and malicious bots... be a good bot. Wikipedia says "Typically, bots perform tasks that are both simple and structurally repetitive, at a much higher rate than would be possible for a human alone."
