Dynamic learning agreement

A responsibility to family and community is what motivates me to take action. The frame of an individual desire to learn and develop self can get in the way because sometimes I have to navigate internalised hate and feel like I don't deserve to become a better person.

In order to succeed, I have to take action and believe that I am a worthy project and that this project of self-awareness and alofa will help me serve my collective responsibility to our ancestors past, here and yet to come.
