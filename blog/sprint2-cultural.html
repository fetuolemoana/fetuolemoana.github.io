Draft learning agreement

A responsibility to family and community is what motivates me to take action. The frame of an individual desire to learn and develop self can get in the way because sometimes I have to navigate internalised hate and feel like I don't deserve to become a better person.

In order to succeed, I have to take action and believe that I am a worthy project and that this project of self-awareness and alofa will help me serve my collective responsibility to our ancestors past, here and yet to come.

Foundations fellowship and responsiveness

I can't engage in helping my foundations whanau during my Auckland Council work schedule. However, I want to make an effort to show-up for my crew during my Dev Academy work schedule times, but I'm not sure how to make this a measurable goal.

My conduct towards my fellow Foundations and wider DEV Academy crew will be respectful and friendly and honour my core Samoan relationship ethics. I will aim to behave in ways that communicate 'the ancestors within and beyond me recognise the ancestors within and beyond you'.
