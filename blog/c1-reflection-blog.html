Quick reflection on Shereef's Kitchen v Table

"We woke up in the kitchen saying, how the hell did this shit happen?" Beyonce

What's your take on the DBC/EDA experience?

I appreciate people that expect to work in the kitchen together. I also appreciate people that know how to rinse and wash dishes. I've heard that if you are not at the table, you're on the menu. I don't want to be on the menu and often I don't like the tables we build as a society. The creator mindset presents the kitchen as an option and I feel that perspective is where I'm at.

What are your impressions?

How do you see yourself engaging with this type of culture?

I have no 'hustle' when it comes to a competitive mindset and I feel like what Bishay had to say about the act of allowing yourself to be vulnerable and expose your ignorance, is on point. Collective growth that nurtures feedback and accountability is exciting to me and I feel that I will thrive in a "team sport" environment. My experience of being in an actual kitchen is about family working together and that's how I hope to be working with my fellow cohortians.
