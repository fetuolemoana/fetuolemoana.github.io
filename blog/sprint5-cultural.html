Foundation core skills reflections

The impact of the core learning on my Foundations learning journey

What surprised me most about my Foundations core learning journey is the practical experience of the idea that personal development is an infinite project.

Three things I have learnt about myself as a result of core learning

  1. I am a worthy project, and so is everyone else.
  2. Consistency and calm is the coconut.
  3. Vulnerability and courageous authenticity is key to me switching from living as surviving vs thriving.

One of the most challenging aspects of the core learning was practicing prioritising my learning and trying to establish consistent self care while dealing with shades of self doubt.

It was also difficult when I got behind in tech, to then feel ready to tackle core from a strengths-based growth perspective. Having a supportive facilitator / mentor who works from a growth mindset by praising a solid process helps me perservere.
