Emotional intelligence

Search Inside Yourself I

The first chapter of Search Inside Yourself by Chade-Meng Tan offered insight about mindfulness and emotional intelligence.

Mindfulness is defined as 'moment to moment non-judgemental awareness' and/or 'bicep curls for the brain'.

Emotional intelligence (EI) is defined as a 'collection of emotional skills'.

Of particular importance is Chade-Meng's assertion that EI is trainable via:

  • attention training e.g. 2 minutes of mindfulness
  • self-knowledge and mastery e.g understanding the differences between existential, experiential and physiological selfhood
  • creating useful mental habits e.g wishing happiness for each person you meet.

I have been practicing meditation and mindfulness since the beginning of the course and I believe it has gradually improved my ability to be present with dev tasks and loved ones. I plan to continue doing my brain bicep curls!
