Meditation process

Search Inside Yourself II

How did the 'process over product' concept affect the way you tackled the site redesign and rebuild?

The idea of process over product reminded me to focus on understanding rather than the look of my blog.

What did you think about meditation before reading chapter 2?

Meditation is something I have always wanted to make time for in my life. I was aware of the benefits, but lacked much practice.

What new things have you learnt about meditation?

I learnt about mindfulness training two strands, attention and meta-attention. I enjoyed reading about meta-attention as attention to attention itself, self-directed kindness (being a loving grandmother) and the four steps to deal with distraction. I learnt a lot about mindfulness meditation in this chapter and found it easy to understand and can see how simply some of the practices can benefit many aspects of our everyday lives.

Did any of the suggested meditation techniques stand out to you?

Imagining that you are your own kind grandmother to achieve self-directed kindness is a great strategy and the four step plan to work with distractions during meditation: 1. Acknowledge; 2. Experience without judging or reacting; 3. If you need to react, continue maintaining mindfulness; 4. Let it go.
