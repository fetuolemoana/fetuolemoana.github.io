Search Inside Yourself

The first chapter of Search Inside Yourself by Chade-Meng Tan offered insight about mindfulness and emotional intelligence.

Mindfulness is defined as 'moment to moment non-judgemental awareness' and / or 'bicep curls for the brain'.

Emotional intelligence (EI) is defined as a 'collection of emotional skills'.

You can build your EI by:

  • attention training e.g. two minutes of mindfulness
  • self-knowledge and mastery e.g. understanding the differences between existential, experiential and physiological selfhood
  • creating kind mental habits e.g. wishing happiness for every person you meet.
