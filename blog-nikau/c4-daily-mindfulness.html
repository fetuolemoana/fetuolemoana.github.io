Daily mindfulness

Search Inside Yourself by Chade-Meng Tan / Chapter three: Mindfulness without butt on cushion

The practice of mindfulness extends beyond sitting meditation and is applicable to every second, minute, hour and day of life. Being present with a moment and / or movement without judgement can be accepted as a gift to the self and others. Meditation is often portrayed as an individualised practice in palagi contexts. Chade-Meng Tan's writing on mindfulness extends beyond the individual and reaches to connect with others as in the practice of 'other directed mindfulness'.

Tan offers the reader both informal and formal ways of exercising mindful listening and conversation, practical and joyful ways to sustain mindfulness practice, and also how to simultaneously master focused and open attention.
